Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required to rent a car?

You need a valid driving license, a government-issued photo ID (like Aadhar, PAN, or Passport), and in some cases, a credit/debit card for security.

Can I rent a car without a driver (self-drive)?

Yes. We offer both self-drive and chauffeur-driven options. For self-drive, you must have a valid driving license and meet our eligibility criteria.

Is fuel included in the rental price?

Typically, fuel is not included. The car is provided with a certain fuel level, and you are expected to return it with the same level.

Can I take the rental car to a different city or state?

Yes, but you may need to inform us in advance. Interstate travel may require additional permits or fees.

Can I extend my booking?

Yes, extension is possible depending on availability. Please request an extension at least a few hours before the end of your booking.

Do you offer long-term rentals or subscriptions?

Yes. We provide weekly, monthly, and corporate leasing options with discounted rates and added benefits.

How do I make a booking?

You can book through our website or by contacting our customer service.
